Stories & Legends

Gustav Klucis

  • In the 1920s the political posters of Klucis’s wife, Valentina Kulagina, were a good deal more popular and in greater demand than his own works.


  • In 1928 Klucis created a series of postcards dedicated to the All-Union Sports and Athletic Contest.These postcards are considered to be the most expensive soviet postcards in the world. A full set of 9 pieces is deemed to be an absolute rarity.


  • Long before the invention of computer design Klucis’s genius revealed itself in his 3D painting and posters, while most of his contemporaries exercised two-dimensional compositions.
