from Tsiolkovsky

The Milky Way contains hundreds of millions of suns and billions of planets where life could potentially emerge. Life in higher organisms is akin to sleep, while life in higher animals, though terrifying from a human perspective, is subjectively unconscious. There are no sons of gods, but there are mature and therefore rational and perfected sons of the cosmos. There is no god-creator, but there is a cosmos that produces suns, planets, and living beings. The planet is the cradle of intelligence, but one cannot indefinitely live in the cradle.
