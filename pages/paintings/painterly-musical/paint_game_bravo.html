As you may have guessed, this painting is related to the theme of space. Throughout his entire artistic career, Mikhail Matiushin focused on capturing the essence of space in his paintings. The foundation of the composition lies in a complex rhythm of swirling and rotating masses, symbolizing the substance of space, its unity, and its diverse array of colors. Brushstrokes of varying sizes and colors create a visual vibration, inducing a shifting effect. Matiushin and his students frequently employed this technique when portraying the pure essence of aerial or underwater space. The rotational movement of the substance correlates with a high musical overtone, akin to celestial music where colors harmonize and intersect, generating a vibration of sound and color.
