Legends

Lubov Popova

Popova’s brother had a stepson who lived in Zvenigorod, outside Moscow. Pavel Sergeievich once suggested me to visit him because he assumed that some paintings of his sister might have remained there. So I went ... It was a country house with a big garden. It was the time then everything was blooming around. Light pink apples, sour cherries... I was welcomed very friendly. The first thing I saw on top of the stairs was a painting of Popova and on the painting there was a craft hanging.


Later we walked to the garden. I noticed that the window of the storage house was nailed with a plywood. On the plywood I could read a number and below the signature: «Popova». I entered the storage house and I saw the other side of the plywood. It was a great painting! «No, I can’t give it to you. If it rains the warehouse will be wet. Bring me a plywood and only then I will give you the painting». I had to go to Moscow and look for a piece of plywood. I didn’t find the dimensions needed so I bought two smaller pieces and brought them to Zvenigorod. In return, the landlord gave me the wonderful painting.
